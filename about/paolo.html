Artist -- ArmorQuest: The Way of the Warrior

Paolo Libunao is an illustrator and concept artist from the Philippines. His background includes developing character designs, producing storyboards, and providing illustrations and sequential art for a wide range of clients and publishers. After working with Paolo on the comic The Thieves through Community Comics, Ben Avery knew Paolo was the artist to bring in on ArmorQuest: The Way of the Warrior. When not using his artistic skills, Paolo serves in a number of ministries in the Philippines.
